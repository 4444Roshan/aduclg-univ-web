the University has 111 affiliated institutes; of these, 87 are self financed and 24 are owned and managed by the Govt. of NCT of Delhi/Govt. of India. In these affiliated institutions, 176 academic programmes are being conducted with an intake of 28,000+ students with a total University strength of 93,000+.
